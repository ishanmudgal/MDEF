# Imagining Alternative Intelligences

What would an Artificial Intelligence be like if it had nothing to do with humans? If it didnt need us, or serve us?
What would an Artificial Intelligence be like if it was collective, and not tied down to the individual?
What would an Artificial Intelligence be like if it was divorced from the idea of life the way we know it?

As a response a group of us imagined the dystopian world of Flux, SCI, and the Weaver. We also created 3 short films that are presented along the way!
